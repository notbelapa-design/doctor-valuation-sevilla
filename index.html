Doctor Marketcap Analysis – Sevilla

This page provides a deeper analysis of the doctor labour market in Seville using the latest demographic reports, salary benchmarks and hospital data. All figures are estimates and should be interpreted as broad indicators rather than exact counts.

Overview

Seville has 9 631 active doctors【282232789000643†L212-L223】, across 13 public hospitals and 10 private hospitals【496329393683654†L194-L215】. Around 79.9 % of registered doctors in Andalusia are in active practice【282232789000643†L212-L223】. Private hospitals provide ~20 % of beds but generate considerable revenue.

Salary Distribution

The salary distribution is heavily skewed by speciality and experience. Top earners – such as dermatologists, gynaecologists and interventional cardiologists – typically earn 100 k–120 k €/year【738407194350164†L596-L604】. Mid‑range specialties like anaesthesiology and traumatology average ~70 k €/year【738407194350164†L596-L604】. The median doctor in Seville earns about 48 k €/year【93213640509497†L20-L36】.
