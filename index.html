Doctor Marketcap Analysis – Seville

A data-driven insight into the medical labour market of Seville.

Overview

Seville has about 9,631 active doctors. The province hosts 13 public hospitals and 10 private hospitals, providing roughly 4,464 beds. Public hospitals account for about 80 % of all beds, while private hospitals focus on high-value specialties.

Salary Distribution

The salary distribution among doctors is skewed by speciality and experience. Top earners (elite specialties) capture a disproportionate share of total payroll, while family and early-career doctors earn considerably less.
